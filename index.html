About The Legacy

Boovanahalli Kantharaju Legacy is a tribute to the values and vision of Late Boovanahalli Kantharaju, whose life was defined by integrity, hard work, and trust. Though he is no longer with us, his principles continue to guide every step we take.

Carrying this legacy forward, we are committed to building a strong and reliable presence in the real estate sector, rooted in transparency and long-term value creation. With a deep understanding of the local market in Hassan and surrounding regions, we aim to provide dependable solutions for both investors and homebuyers.

Through our platform, Vrishabha Properties, we offer verified listings, honest guidance, and complete support — from site visits to registration. Our goal is not just to close transactions, but to build lasting relationships based on trust.

At B K Legacy, we honor the past while building for the future — combining traditional values with a modern approach to real estate, development, and investment.

Our Divisions

At B K Legacy, our operations are structured into specialized divisions, each focused on delivering excellence across different areas of real estate and business. This integrated approach allows us to provide end-to-end solutions while maintaining high standards of quality, transparency, and long-term value creation.

Vrishabha Properties

Vrishabha Properties serves as our official property listing and brokerage platform, offering a curated portfolio of verified residential and commercial properties.

We assist clients through every stage of the transaction — including property selection, market analysis, price negotiation, legal verification, and registration support — ensuring a smooth and transparent experience.

  • Verified Property Listings
  • Site Visits & Consultation
  • End-to-End Transaction Support

BK Legacy Developments

Our development division focuses on planning and executing premium layouts and land development projects in high-growth locations.

We emphasize strategic planning, infrastructure development, and long-term value appreciation, ensuring that every project meets modern standards and future investment potential.

  • Future-Ready Projects

Business & Investments
