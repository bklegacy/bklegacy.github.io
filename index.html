Our Story

About The Legacy

Boovanahalli Kantharaju Legacy is a tribute to the values and vision of Late Boovanahalli Kantharaju, whose life was defined by integrity, hard work, and trust. Though he is no longer with us, his principles continue to guide every step we take.

Carrying this legacy forward, we are committed to building a strong and reliable presence in the real estate sector, rooted in transparency and long-term value creation. With a deep understanding of the local market in Hassan and surrounding regions, we aim to provide dependable solutions for both investors and homebuyers.

Through our platform, Vrishabha Properties, we offer verified listings, honest guidance, and complete support — from site visits to registration. Our goal is not just to close transactions, but to build lasting relationships based on trust.

Inspiration Behind the Name

The name "B K Legacy" is a tribute to Sri Boovanahalli Kantharaju. He was a farmer, involved in real estate, and active in public life and politics. His values continue to inspire the vision behind our brand.

His life — rooted in the soil, guided by integrity, and dedicated to his community — remains the living compass of everything we build at B K Legacy. We honour his memory by upholding the standards he set and carrying his vision forward.
